Bulimia

Scroll down for more information.

More

What it is

When a person consumes or binges food, then after eating the food feel obligated to make themselves throw up, or get rid of the food through laxatives.

Next

Causes

  • 1. Genetic
  • 2. Society making people believe it is a necessary to be thin
  • 3. Low self esteem
  • 4. Major and stressful changes
  • 5. Work in an appearance based workplace
Next

Symptoms

  • 1. Eating food but staying thin
  • 2. Going to the bathroom after almost too all meals
  • 3. Takes pills to lose weight
  • 4. May smell like vomit after coming from the bathroom
  • 5. Works out excessively especially after eating
  • 6. Has discolored teeth
  • 7. Has a fluctuating weight
  • 8. Scars or calluses on the knuckles or hand
  • 9. Not underweight
  • 10. Rarely eats normal meals either eats a large amount or a too little
Next

Effects

  • 1. Weight gain
  • 2. Abdominal pain
  • 3. Bloating of the stomach
  • 4. Hoarseness or sore throat
  • 5. Swollen checks
  • 6. Weak
  • 7. Dizzy
  • 8. Tooth Decay
  • 9. Swelling of the hands and feet
  • 10. Mouth sores
Next

Support

It is highly recommended that someone struggling with bulimia go to a therapist.
