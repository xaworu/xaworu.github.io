What Is Anorexia?

When a person avoids eating or does healthy exercise to the point where they are burning off more calories than they are taking in.

Next

Causes

  • 1. Genetics
  • 2. Family putting pressure on be thin
  • 3. Usually a perfections or overachievers
  • 4. Society making people believe it is a necessary to be thin
Next

Symptoms

  • 1. Diets even when they are thin
  • 2. Obsession with calories intake
  • 3. Pretend to eat or hide throwing away food
  • 4. Dramatic weight loss
  • 5. Fixed on what their body should look like
  • 6. Critical of their body image
  • 7. Denial of being thin
  • 8. Exercises through unhealthy conditions
  • 9. Throwing up after eating
  • 10. Using diet pills to an unhealthy extent
Next

Effects

  • 1. Severe mood swings
  • 2. Lack of energy
  • 3. Depression
  • 4. Weakness
  • 5. Slow thinking
  • 6. Dry, yellowish skin and brittle nails
  • 7. Bloating
  • 8. Tooth decay and gum damage
  • 9. Dizziness and gum damage
  • 10. Headaches
Next

Support

It is highly recommended that someone struggling with anorexia go to a therapist.
