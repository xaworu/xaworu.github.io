Binge Eating

Scroll down for more information.

More

What it is

When a person has no control over how much food they eat and then consumes a lot of food to the point where they throw up

Next

Causes

  • 1.Genetic
  • 2.Society making people believe it is a necessary to be thin
  • 3.When depressed or have been depressed
Next

Symptoms

  • 1. Inability to stop eating or control when eating
  • 2. Hiding or stockpiling food to eat for later
  • 3. Eating even when you are full
  • 4. Eating normally around other, but gorging when alone
  • 5. Gets stress when talks or thinks about eating
  • 6. Embarrassed about the amount of food consumed
  • 7. Feels guilty, disgusted or depressed after overeating
  • 8. Wants to control their weight or diet
  • 9. Eating large amount of food rapidly
  • 10. Feels numb while consuming food
Next

Effects

  • 1. Type 2 diabetes
  • 2. Gallbladder disease
  • 3. High cholesterol
  • 4. High blood pressure
  • 5. Heart disease
  • 6. Certain types of cancer
  • 7. Osteoarthritis
  • 8. Joint and muscle pain
  • 9. Gastrointestinal problems
  • 10. Sleep apnea
Next

Support

It is highly recommended that someone struggling with binge eating go to a therapist.
