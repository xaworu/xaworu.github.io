ADHD

Attention Deficit Hyeractivity Disoder

When a person has hyperactivity, difficulty paying attention, and impulsiveness, because of this it makes it harder for them to get projects done and stay on task.

Scroll down for more information.

More

Effects

  • 1. Disorganized
  • 2. Lacks Focus
  • 3. Doesn’t listen when people talk
  • 4. Get distracted easily
  • 5. Impatience
  • 6. Squirms or fidgets when seated
  • 7. Has low self esteem
  • 8. Forgetful
  • 9. Blurts out or interrupts
  • 10. Trouble with doing tasks quietly
Next

Support

1. Look them in the eye

  • When you look them in the eye it allows them to focus and will try harder to listen.

2. Make deadlines before original deadline

  • This is good because it gives them more time to accomplish the task in time.

3. Writes important things in down

  • It is important to write things down for them because it then reminds them that they need to do that task.
Next

Tips

  • 1. Have a stress ball
  • 2. Use different colors when writing things down
  • 3. Set up a daily reminder that reminds you to do tasks throughout the day
  • 4. To have strict schedule or routine that they do everyday
  • 5. To include them in an activity that they enjoy that relaxes or calms them

ADHD vs. ADD

ADD is very similar to ADHD however, they are not impulsive as well as not hyperactive. They still have problems focusing and listening to others, however they are more timid and don’t move around or interrupt as much.
